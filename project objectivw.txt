Project Objective
To reduce frauds during freelancing, we are come up with the idea that there will be a online platform PayThentic which will act as a wise mediator between users. The whole process will be divided into four stages that are Initial, Building, Uploded, and Review.
	
Functionality

* It will let you find users with appropriate username.
* It will provide authentication and authorization of user.
* There will be a feature to upload and download project files.
* User can view his or others profile and can update his profile info.
